Episode I

A New Hope

It is a period of computer war. Bastien, the rebel student, is striking from a hidden base and has won his first victory against the evil council of BeCode.

During the battle, Bastien's spies managed to steal secret plans to the Empire’s ultimate weapon, the DEATH MIKE, an armored space guy with enough power to destroy an entire planet.

Pursued by Emily’s sinister agents, Princess Sèni races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her BeCode class and restore freedom to the galaxy of coding academies….
